Innovating Godda

ABOUT

Innovating Godda is a community with an ambition to glorify Godda.

We conduct regular events like Tasveer, Monthly Quizzes, Seminars, Guest Lectures, and multiple events on a regular basis. To enhance the proficiency of the people, Innovating Godda is looking forward to conducting workshops and perform career counselling.

We work with a vision to boost Godda by enriching its people in an innovative manner. In the near future, we intend to organize an annual techno-cultural fest.

BLOG

1.Why to visit Godda🤔?
👉See Godda has countable tourist spots but there are other reasons too for visiting Godda. Like it's street foods (chur-mur,litti-chokha, samosas,etc...) It's AQI(Air Quality Index) which is far better than Delhi. If u want to experience a mixture of village and town visit Godda. If u forgot ur culture and tradition visit the villages of Godda. Aur ab village aa hi gye ho to Dahi Chuda Kaise chor site ho, Try that with jaggery & Banana. We swear u will feel like paradise.


2.How are the people in Godda & their way of living🤔?
👉In Godda people are good-hearted & genuine and prefer to live a peaceful life. And also they are ambitious, Ambitious about what? Ambitious abt Godda that one-day Godda will be one of the developed districts and it will have its railway station that's been the prior ambition of every Goddawasi. 😄 And people (both males & females) here are literate too.
From a child's perspective, Godda is a lovely place & prefers Gandhi maidan & Block field as their destination for cricket😊.
From an adult's point of view, Godda is a developing district and a peaceful place. And loves to hang out with his local friends at a local tea stall.😎 And there are many different types of people that u can't even imagine.
If u want to find your type please visit Godda once 🙌
